GROW GUARD - Smart Irrigation System

Introduction :

Smart irrigation technology uses weather data or soil moisture data to determine the irrigation need of the landscape. Smart irrigation technology includes: These products maximize irrigation efficiency by reducing water waste, while maintaining plant health and quality.Soil moisture sensor-based smart irrigation controllers use one of several well-established technologies to measure soil moisture content. When buried in the root zone of turf, trees or shrubs, the sensors accurately determine the moisture level in the soil and transmit this reading to the controller.

Components used :

  1. Soil moisture sensor
  2. Arduino - WEMOSE D1
  3. OLED display
  4. Motor pump
  5. Motor drivers
  6. Jumper wires

Conclusion :

The smart irrigation system using soil moisture sensors and motor controls represents a transformative solution for sustainable agriculture and water management. By integrating simple yet effective IoT technology, this project addresses critical global challenges while demonstrating practical innovation. This project demonstrates that affordable technology ($20-$100 basic setup) can solve pressing environmental challenges. From drought-stricken farms in India to smart city landscaping in Singapore, automated irrigation systems showcase how technology bridges the gap between ecological responsibility and agricultural productivity. As climate change intensifies water scarcity, these systems will transition from "innovative solutions" to "essential infrastructure" worldwide.
